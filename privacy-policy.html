Eyezen — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

Eyezen is a browser extension that provides eye training exercises and break reminders. Your privacy is important — this extension is designed to work entirely on your device.

Data Collection

Eyezen does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.

Local Storage

The extension stores the following preferences locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Break reminder interval (e.g. 20, 30, 60 minutes)
  • Sound preferences (ambient volume, gong volume, mute state)
  • Timestamp of your last exercise session

This data never leaves your browser and is only used to provide the extension's functionality.

Permissions

  • storage — Save your preferences locally
  • alarms — Schedule break reminders
  • notifications — Show break reminder notifications
